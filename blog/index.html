I Go With Hugo

How I got Hugo Static Site Generator up and running.

April 30, 2016 Ruth Earle

4 minute read

So I finally have my blog up and running. Part of the delay was that I could not find a platform with templates that I liked the other part was sheer laziness. Then I found Hugo. Hugo is written in Go and is blazingly fast at building static content. There are beautiful templates to get you started and it was relatively easy to get everything onto Github. The website states ‘Hugo is quite possibly the easiest to install…, simply download and run’.
